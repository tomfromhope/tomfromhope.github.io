About

North Wales Ecology is led by Amanda Davies, a consultant with experience in protected species, habitat management and planning support. We combine scientific rigour with practical, buildable solutions.

  • Member of CIEEM
  • Fully insured & DBS checked
  • Based in North Wales – working across Wales & the North West

Typical clients

  • Homeowners & architects
  • Small developers & planners
  • Community projects & conservation groups

Services

Protected Species Surveys

Bats, great crested newt, reptiles, badger, otter and birds. Licensed surveyors, seasonally appropriate methods.

Preliminary Ecological Appraisal (PEA)

Baseline habitat assessment, constraints & opportunities, and clear next steps for planning applications.

Ecological Impact Assessment (EcIA)

Impact analysis with pragmatic mitigation and enhancement proposals aligned to local policy.

Method Statements & Supervision

Construction-phase advice, watching briefs, and toolbox talks to keep projects compliant and on schedule.

Recent highlights

Barn conversion, Conwy

Bat surveys and licence support enabling a heritage-led conversion with integrated roost features.

School habitat plan, Gwynedd

PEA and habitat improvement plan creating nature-rich outdoor learning areas.
